About this site

World Leadle

World Leadle is a browser-based geography game by Obliqua Data. Players guess the country of a current world leader and receive hints based on continent, distance, corruption range, and role.

The site is intended as an educational game. It does not ask for a login, a password, phone details, payment details, or other sensitive personal information.

Leader and schedule data are generated from public sources and published as static files through GitHub Pages.
